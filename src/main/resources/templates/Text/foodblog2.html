Introduction

Jee

Bibimbap is a typical Korean dish that mixes a variety of vegetables, meat or egg, and red pepper sauce together. The bibimbap, which is so popular and famous that even celebrities including Michael Jackson love, features traditional Korean food. The basics of Korean food are the balanced composition of the ingredients and the quality that takes a long time to complete. Although there are a lot of ingredients and it takes a long time from purchasing to preparing materials, I would like to introduce to you the food representative of well-being. Especially, I want to eat this bibimbap with you, which can save even the palate of those who have lost their appetite or dislike vegetables. Chef Baek Jongwon uses a lot of seasonal ingredients or meat toppings, but today I will make my own traditional bibimbap inspired by Chef Baek Jongwon and my mom's recipe. there is nothing human cannot do.I graduated from university, and I worked for a public company. After I got married, my life changed dramatically. I am living so busy with my husband and children, my present life is absolutely not quiet, and my family comes before me now.

Instructions

1.Spinach

the cooking utensil has to be prepared some like water boiler, frying pan. Now I will boil the water after the water boils, I add Spinach with a little salt and blanch it (about 2 minutes). And I rinse blanched spinach in cold water, after that I cut to about 3 to 4cm in length. And after adding a little bit of sesame oil and salt and please mix it.

2.Bean sprouts

I put water and bean sprouts in a pot. And after adding a little salt in a pot, I boil it with opening the lid for 2-3 minutes. And after rinsing bean sprouts in cold water, I add a little salt and mix it.

3.Shiitake mushroom

I chop shiitake mushrooms, and then I put the chopped shiitake mushrooms with cooking oil, salt, and water in a frying pan and stir-fry them quickly over under high heat. (for about 1 minute).

4. Carrot

I chop the carrot, after that, I put the chopped carrot with cooking oil in a frying pan and stir-fry them quickly over under high heat. (for about 1 minute).

5. Lettuce

I Chop the lettuce into 3~4cm long pieces

6. Seaweed

I Chop the seaweed into 3~4cm long pieces.

7. Fried Eggs

I fry an egg with a frying pan

Who is he?

JongWon Baek is known as a talented Korean chef, He is on the Entertainment Television program called Baek Jongwon's Alley Restaurant. It is a South Korean cooking-variety program that has been broadcast since January 5, 2018. It is a spin-off of the cooking-variety program Baek Jongwon's Top 3 Chef King. It is currently hosted by JongWon Baek and SeongJu Kim, it aired on SBS: Seoul Broadcasting System every Friday at 23:20 KST: Korea Standard Time. 

                                           

Why we choose him?

JongWon Baek grew up in a rich family, entered a top university in South Korea, and was always in a stable environment, but all he wanted, was not economic stability but making tasty food. For example, JongWon Baek, who graduated from the Department of Social Work (not Culinary) had a very interesting university life. Instead of studying, he traveled across the country in search of tasty restaurants, which helped him accumulate an extensive and comprehensive knowledge of food. He would often cook for his classmates and soon became a central figure in his department. JongWon Baek, enjoy eating and likes friendly, simple, but delicious rice and food more than expensive and complex luxury dishes. It is Korean homemade food. It is a home-made meal including a mother's finger touch and heart. It is the reason we choose him.

His value

In a Television program called Baek JongWon' Alley Restaurant, JongWon Baek visits a food alley where self-employed people struggle with that business has been slow and renews them from store interior to food and other business skills. With his help, many self-employed people have actually achieved great success in the food business. He is a savior of the South Korean diet. He is worth more than a cook.
